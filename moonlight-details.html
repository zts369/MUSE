Moonlight Room

Indulge in comfort and sophistication in our tastefully appointed Moonlight Room.

Spanning 60 sqm, the Moonlight Room offers a private haven with stylish features and furnishings. Designed for ultimate relaxation, the space includes a king-size bed with premium Sealy mattresses, a spacious bathroom featuring a deep-soak bathtub, a refreshing rain shower, and exclusive designer amenities. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway or a quiet retreat, this room provides the perfect blend of modern luxury and cozy ambiance.
